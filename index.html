Verse of the Day

(Psalm 139:13–16 ESV) You are fearfully and wonderfully made. Your days were written before one began.

RiseXpressionzz Devotional — Day 1

Reflection: How will you view today’s challenges knowing your life is part of God’s pre-written plan?

Prayer

Dear Lord, I thank you for writing my story before time began. Help me to rest in the assurance that Your plans for me are good. Let my heart be anxious for nothing and replace my uncertainty with a deep, abiding faith in Your sovereign will for my life. Amen.

📱 How to Save the D.E.E.D Bible App

On Android (Samsung, Huawei, etc.)

  1. Open the app link in Chrome: https://risebeyondministry.github.io/D.E.E.D-Bible-App/
  2. Tap the three dots ⋮ in the top‑right corner.
  3. Select Add to Home screen.
  4. Tap Add → the app icon will appear on your home screen.

On iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open the app link in Safari: https://risebeyondministry.github.io/D.E.E.D-Bible-App/
  2. Tap the Share button ⬆️ (square with arrow at the bottom).
  3. Scroll down and select Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add → the app icon will appear on your home screen.

Tip: Once saved, the app opens like a regular mobile app with the D.E.E.D logo as its icon.
